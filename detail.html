Black Pink

"Shut Down" is a song recorded by South Korean girl group Blackpink. It was released on September, 16, 2022 through YG Entertainment
and Interscope Records as the second single of the studio album, Born Pink (2022). It is primarily a hip hop song driven by looped clasical
violin, trap beats, string and bass sounds. It was written by its producer 24, alongside Teddy, Danny Chung, and Vince.
